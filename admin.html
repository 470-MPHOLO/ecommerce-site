How to Get Imgur Image URLs:

  1. Go to imgur.com/upload
  2. Drag & drop your image or click to upload
  3. After upload, right-click the image
  4. Select "Copy image address"
  5. Paste the URL in the field below
Correct format: https://i.imgur.com/abc123.jpg
(NOT https://imgur.com/gallery/...)
Make sure it starts with https://i.imgur.com/
